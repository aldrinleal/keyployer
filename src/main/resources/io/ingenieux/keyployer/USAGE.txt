keyployer

Usage:
  keyployer (--fqdn <fqdn>) (--signed <signed>) (--truststore <truststore>) (--cmhost <cmhost>) (--hue-host <huehost>)

Options:
  -h --help                Show this screen.
  --version                Show version.
  --fqdn <fqdn>            This hosts' fqdn.
  --cmhost <cmhost>        CM Host FQDN
  --signed <sk>            [Self-] Signed Keystore.
  --truststore <ts>        Truststore
  --hue-host <hue-host>    Include hue?
