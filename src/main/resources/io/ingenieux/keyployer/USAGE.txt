keyployer

Usage:
  keyployer (--fqdn <fqdn>) (--signed <signed>) (--truststore <truststore>) (--hosts-truststore <hosts-truststore>) (--cmhost=<cmhost>) (--hue-host=<huehost>)

Options:
  -h --help                Show this screen.
  --version                Show version.
  --fqdn=<fqdn>            This hosts' fqdn.
  --cmhost=<cmhost>        CM Host FQDN
  --signed=<sk>            (Self-) Signed Keystore.
  --truststore=<ts>        Truststore
  --hosts-truststore=<hts> Hosts Truststore
  --hue-host=<hue-host>    Include hue?
